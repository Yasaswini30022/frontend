Authorization

Triumph Travels is one of the leading tour operators and travel agents. The company also works in association with prime national agencies in the tourism industry such as Indian Association of Tour Operators (IATO) and Indian Mountaineering Foundation. These memberships are a proof of the credibility of our services in the business. We are associated with:

Department of Tourism: It is the axis of tourism in India that formulates the rules and regulations and directs various central and state travel agencies and private sector tour operators to develop and promote tourism in our country.

International Air Transport Association (IATA )- It is an association in the aviation industry that controls the international air traffic prioritizing safety, security and hospitality and thus simplifying the business. It introduced the concept of electronic ticket and bar coded boarding pass in 2004.

Travel Operators for Tigers (TOFT): It is an initiation to develop and manage the wildlife tourism in India and internationally with specific guidelines for operators, service providers and travellers. On the other hand TOFT also practices in enhancing wildlife conservation and community support. - Find us on TOFT Official Website

Indian Association of Tour Operators (IATO) - A trade body of inbound Indian Tour Operators who work hard for offering excellent services to the visitors. - Find us on IATO Official Website
